About Us

Welcome to Rishton Academy Primary School, where we are committed to providing a nurturing and inspiring learning environment for our students. Our school is a place where every child is valued, and their individual talents and abilities are recognized and celebrated. We believe that education is the key to unlocking a child's full potential, and we strive to provide a well-rounded education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also fosters personal growth, social responsibility, and emotional well-being. At Rishton Academy Primary School, we are dedicated to helping our students develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in an ever-changing world.
